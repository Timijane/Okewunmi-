Okewunmi Dynasty - About

The Okewunmi Children

Oshode Damilola (née Mama Oshode)

The firstborn of the family, Damilola, stands as a model of resilience, wisdom, and industriousness. A graduate of Computer Science from Ire Ekiti Polytechnic, she has carved a niche in the business world as a leather materials mogul. She is happily married to Mr. Oshode Damilare, and their union is blessed with two children, Ademide and Iremide.

Engr. Okewunmi Ayomide

The second-born and first son, Ayomide, is a skilled engineer specializing in heavy-duty machine repairs. He is working on launching “Okewunmi Autos” to redefine the automobile industry. He is married to Simisola Okewunmi, and they are blessed with a son, Simon Okewunmi.

Okewunmi Oluwagbenga

The third-born, Oluwagbenga, embodies the entrepreneurial creativity of the Okewunmi family. With a passion for style and innovation, he has ventured into the shoe and fashion industry, where his designs and business ideas continue to flourish. Beyond his entrepreneurial journey, Oluwagbenga is a family man, happily married and dedicated to building a home founded on love, respect, and godly values.

Okewunmi Opeyemi (Mrs. Mathew)

The fourth-born, Opeyemi, is a woman of elegance, intelligence, and godliness. She thrives in the fabric and textile industry and is happily married to Pastor Mathew. Together, they are raising a godly family, continuing the legacy of faith that runs deep in the Okewunmi bloodline.

Pastor Okewunmi Abayomi (Yomi)

The fifth-born, fondly called Yomi, stands out as both a scholar and a minister of the Gospel. He graduated with Distinction in Insurance from The Polytechnic Ibadan and later earned First-Class honors from the University of Ibadan. Today, he serves as Senior Pastor at Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church, Ibadan Headquarters, and is also the founder of Edu Top Consultant, an academic platform that empowers students. Yomi is happily married to Dr. (Mrs.) Kehinde Okewunmi, a medical doctor and pastor, and they are blessed with a son. His life reflects the balance of spiritual leadership, academic excellence, and family devotion.

Okewunmi Motunrayo (Mrs. Lucky Aba)

The sixth-born, Motunrayo, is a shining example of beauty, talent, and versatility. A graduate of Adekunle Ajasin University and a trained Laboratory Scientist, she has made her mark as a fashionista, NGO founder, minister of the Word, and skilled entrepreneur. Motunrayo is admired for her elegance and leadership. She is happily married to Mr. Lucky Aba, a man of vision and character, and they are blessed with a son. Her life reflects grace, excellence, and impact across different spheres.

Okewunmi Timileyin

The seventh-born, Timileyin, is a man of intellect, creativity, and vision. A graduate of Counseling Psychology from Olabisi Onabanjo University (OOU), he has become a renowned educator and founder of Triangle of Scholars, an educational platform that nurtures academic excellence. He is also a poet, novelist, software developer, cybersecurity graduate, and media director at Kingdom Citizens Church International. Though unmarried, he is in a promising relationship. His journey reflects the dynamism and modern edge of the Okewunmi Dynasty.

Okewunmi Pelumi Paul

The last-born, Pelumi Paul, is a powerhouse of vision, innovation, and determination. Currently a law student at Kola Daisi University, Ibadan, he is also a tech-driven entrepreneur, Amazon affiliate marketer, poet, novelist, motivational speaker, and online trader. Known as the youngest motivational speaker in Africa, he inspires countless lives through his platform, Upphillan TV. His determination and creativity highlight the enduring strength of the Okewunmi family legacy.
