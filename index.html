Welcome to the Okewunmi Dynasty

The Okewunmi Dynasty is a family rooted in truth, sacrifice, faith, and legacy. At the heart of this great family is Elder Solomon Okewunmi, a man of integrity, wisdom, and devotion.

Now at the age of 71 (in 2025), Elder Solomon stands as a pillar of strength. By his side is Deaconess Adebola Okewunmi, a virtuous woman and nurturing mother. Together, they built a home of love, discipline, and godliness.

The Okewunmi Children

Oshode Damilola (née Mama Oshode)

The firstborn of the family, Damilola, stands as a model of resilience and industriousness. She studied Computer Science at Ire Ekiti Polytechnic and became a leather materials mogul. She is happily married to Mr. Oshode Damilare, and their union is blessed with two children: Ademide and Iremide.

Engr. Okewunmi Ayomide

The second-born and first son, Ayomide, is a skilled engineer in heavy-duty machine repairs. He is launching “Okewunmi Autos” to redefine the automobile industry. He is married to Simisola Okewunmi, and they are blessed with a son, Simon Okewunmi.

Okewunmi Oluwagbenga

The third-born, Oluwagbenga, embodies entrepreneurial creativity in the shoe and fashion industry. With boldness and innovation, he blends tradition with modern trends. He is happily married and blessed with children, continuing the Okewunmi family legacy.

Okewunmi Opeyemi (Mrs. Mathew)

The fourth-born, Opeyemi, is a woman of elegance and intelligence. A graduate of the University of Lagos, she thrives in the fabric and textile industry. She is happily married to Pastor Mathew, and together they are raising a godly family.

Pastor Okewunmi Abayomi (Yomi)

The fifth-born, Yomi, is both a scholar and minister. He graduated with Distinction in Insurance from The Polytechnic Ibadan and later with First-Class honors from UI. Today, he pastors at Salvation Proclaimers Anointed Church and leads Edu Top Consultant. He is married to Dr. Kehinde Okewunmi, and they are blessed with a son.

Okewunmi Motunrayo (Mrs. Lucky Aba)

The sixth-born, Motunrayo, is a graduate of Adekunle Ajasin University and a trained Laboratory Scientist. She is also a fashionista, NGO founder, minister, and entrepreneur. She is happily married to Mr. Lucky Aba, and they are blessed with a son.

Okewunmi Timileyin

The seventh-born, Timileyin, is a graduate of Counseling Psychology from OOU. He is the founder of Triangle of Scholars, a poet, novelist, software developer, cybersecurity graduate, and media director at Kingdom Citizens Church. Though unmarried, he is in a promising relationship.

Okewunmi Pelumi Paul

The last-born, Pelumi Paul, is a law student at Kola Daisi University, Ibadan. He is also a tech entrepreneur, Amazon affiliate marketer, poet, novelist, motivational speaker, and founder of Upphillan TV. Known as the youngest motivational speaker in Africa, his life inspires many.
