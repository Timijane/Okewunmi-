About - Okewunmi Solomon Dynasty

Welcome to the Okewunmi Dynasty

The Okewunmi Dynasty is a family rooted in truth, faith, sacrifice, and legacy. At the heart of this great family is Elder Solomon Okewunmi, a man widely respected for his integrity, wisdom, and selfless devotion. Born and raised in Ogun State, Egba, Abeokuta—Itori Oke, Elder Solomon exemplifies a life of service to both family and community.

Now at the age of 71 (in 2025), Elder Solomon continues to stand as a pillar of strength and guidance. By his side is his beloved wife, Deaconess Adebola Okewunmi, a virtuous woman and nurturing mother. Together, they have built a strong family rooted in love, discipline, and godliness.

The Okewunmi Children

Oshode Damilola

The firstborn, Damilola, is a model of resilience and wisdom. A Computer Science graduate, she is a successful entrepreneur in the leather industry. Married to Mr. Oshode Damilare, they are blessed with two children, Ademide and Iremide.

Engr. Okewunmi Ayomide

The second-born and first son, Ayomide, is a skilled engineer launching “Okewunmi Autos.” Married to Simisola Okewunmi, they are blessed with a son, Simon Okewunmi.

Okewunmi Oluwagbenga

The third-born, Oluwagbenga, is an entrepreneur in the shoe and fashion industry. Married with a family, he blends tradition with modern trends in his business ventures.

Okewunmi Opeyemi

The fourth-born, Opeyemi, excels in the fabric and textile industry. Married to Pastor Mathew, she continues the legacy of faith and excellence.
